INAV 9.0.0-RC2 is here!
We're excited to announce the first release candidate of INAV 9.0! This release brings major improvements including enhanced position estimation, fixed-wing altitude controller options, temperature compensation for sensors, VTOL tilt-rotor enhancements, and 30+ new flight controller targets.
A massive thank you to everyone who made this possible:

44 code contributors to INAV firmware
14 contributors to INAV Configurator
Our dedicated testers like Jetrell who put countless hours into validation
The community members providing tech support on Discord and Facebook
Everyone who reported bugs, wrote documentation, and helped fellow pilots

This is truly a community effort. ❤️
Get it here:
Firmware: https://github.com/iNavFlight/inav/releases/tag/9.0.0-RC2
Configurator: https://github.com/iNavFlight/inav-configurator/releases/tag/9.0.0-RC1
